How to Cook Food in Grow a Garden

Cooking in Grow a Garden is simple but requires the right ingredients and timing. Here's how to get started:

ðŸ¥•Step-by-Step Cooking Instructions

1

Gather Ingredients

Collect the correct fruits or vegetables needed for the recipe you want to make. You can find or grow these in your garden.

2

Head to the Cooking Area

Go to the newly added cooking section in the lobby.

3

Add Ingredients to the Pot

Hold the correct item and walk up to the Cooking Pot. Press the E key to place the ingredient inside. The board to the left will display the cooking time based on the ingredients you have added.

4

Start Cooking

Once all required ingredients are in the pot, press the green COOK button. The pot will display a timer above it. Wait until it finishes.

5

Collect the Dish

Once the cooking is complete, approach the pot again and press E to collect your food item.

ðŸ’¡

Pro Tip

Different ingredient combinations will create different dishes with varying cooking times. Experiment with different recipes to discover new dishes and their rewards!
